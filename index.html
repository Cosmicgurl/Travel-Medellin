Why Medellín

The city of eternal spring

Medellín is worth a visit for so many reasons! This city offers a unique blend of culture, adventure, and natural beauty. Whether you're a culture enthusiast, an adrenaline junkie, or a nature lover, Medellin has something special for you. For me personally its the people, the weather, the culture and the way they invite you to learn more about Colombias history.

Best places to get to know Medellín

Comuna 13

Comuna 13 or San Javier is one of the 16 communes of the city of Medellín, Colombia, with a population of around 160,000. The neighborhood is associated with street art performances, graffiti, bright colors, tours, and an energetic environment that showcases its resilience

Address:

#34cc-2 a 34cc-58, Cra. 110, Medellín, Antioquia, Colombia

What I like about it

It´s actually everything. I love the street art, the flair, the diffrent artists, the food..

El Peñón de Guatapé

The Rock of Guatapé is a landmark inselberg in Colombia. It is located in the town and municipality of Guatapé, Antioquia. You ca climb it within 20 minutes and enjoy an amazing view from up there.

Address:

Vereda la piedra, Guatape, Guatapé, Antioquia, Colombia

What I like about it

The 702 stairs in the "zipper" of the rock are fun to climb plus the view was breathtaking

Learn more

Plaza Botero

Botero Plaza, surrounded by the Museum of Antioquia and the Rafael Uribe Uribe Palace of Culture, is a 7,000 m² outside park that displays 23 sculptures by Colombian artist Fernando Botero, who donated these and several other artworks for the museum's renovation in 2004.

Address:

Av. Carabobo, La Candelaria, Medellín, La Candelaria, Medellín, Antioquia, Colombia

What I like about it

It is impressive, it is super interesting once you read more into Boteros art and it is free.
